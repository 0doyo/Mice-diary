ABOUT MICE DIARY

MICE Diary is a platform dedicated to Kenya’s Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions (MICE) industry. It provides insightful content, research, and industry updates, tailored specifically for key stakeholders in the Kenyan MICE ecosystem. The platform highlights the latest trends, emerging issues, and innovations in the events sector, while also promoting collaboration among event organizers, venues, and tourism entities. MICE Diary's goal is to become a leading resource for the industry by offering locally relevant content, such as showcasing successful corporate events and national events and fostering partnerships with key institutions promoting the MICE Agenda. The MICE Diary is a privately owned initiative by Caspi Public Relations Limited.

Our Mission

1. Foster innovation and growth in the events industry.

2. Provide comprehensive industry insights and research.

3. Address and highlight emerging issues within the MICE ecosystem.

4. Bridge the information gap and support stakeholders in making informed decisions.

What Inspired Us

The Untimely Dissolution of the Kenya National Convention Bureau (KNCB), Rwanda MICE Tourism Strategy, Semi-Professional Kenyan labour market and Declarations from the Global MICE Summits which championed for heightened stakeholder coordination and Lack of information sharing in the MICE Sector. Till to date there is no baseline survey done and Open Source Industry information which derails Innovations.

Why the MICE Diary Important

1. Rapid Growth of the MICE IndustryGlobally, the MICE industry is valued at over $900 billion and is expected to grow by nearly 12% by 2028. In Kenya, MICE contribute significantly to the tourism and business sectors, with Nairobi ranked as one of Africa’s top MICE destinations by the International Congress and Convention Association (ICCA). MICE Diary taps into this growing market to provide essential insights.

2. Key Contributor to Kenya’s Economy:The MICE sector is a vital component of Kenya’s economy, contributing to job creation, foreign exchange earnings, and boosting local businesses, including hospitality and transportation. MICE Diary helps stakeholders keep up with trends and capitalize on these opportunities.

3. Localizing Industry Information: There is limited content tailored specifically to the Kenyan MICE market. MICE Diary bridges this gap by offering localized insights, addressing unique challenges, and promoting events that drive Kenya’s MICE growth.

4. Corporate Interest in MICE Events:Major companies in Kenya, are increasingly investing in corporate events like golf tournaments, Awards and conferences, Summits to engage stakeholders. MICE Diary showcases these events, encouraging more organizations to participate and grow the sector.

5. Partnership and Collaboration: MICE Diary provides a platform for partnerships with key industry players offering them visibility while enriching the content for readers. These media partnerships are essential for advancing Kenya's MICE potential.

6. Promotion of Kenya as a MICE Destination: Kenya’s strategic location and world class infrastructure, such as the KICC and Bomas of Kenya, make it a prime MICE destination. MICE Diary promotes these venues and helps position Kenya on the global stage.
