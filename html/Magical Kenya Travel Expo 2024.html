Magical Kenya Travel Expo 2024 to position Kenya as a preferred destination in Africa

September 12, 2024

Magical Kenya travel Expo

The most anticipated premium Business-to-business event platform for tourism investors and industry players will kick start on 2nd October to 4th October 2024 at the Uhuru Gardens dubbed The Magical Kenya Travel Expo 2024. This will be the 14th Edition of the Event organized by Kenya Tourism Board., managed by Events and Promotions Limited which was awarded the Open Tender to Manage Exposition for the Period 2023/2024.

For this year’s Expo Kenya Tourism Board Over 5000 Delegates compared to last year which garnered 3000 Delegates. The MKTE 2024 will have a wide arrear of exhibitors from different sectors in tourism from Accommodation, DMCs – travel agents and tour operators, Tourism Boards, Counties & government agencies, Technology, Transport providers, Signature Experiences, MICE, Tourism Affiliated Services, Destination Marketing, to M.S.M.E. Kenya is targeting new frontiers markets closer to home such as Brazil, Mexico; Asia – Saudi Arabia, Qatar, United Arab Emirates and Australia in efforts to increase it’s tourist arrivals, this year Kenya has a target of 3 million arrivals which 41% accounts for international arrivals.

The Expo is a great marketing tool for Kenya to position itself as a preferred destination in Africa. About 60% of SMEs will get an opportunity to get access to the global markets and forge business contacts with other foreign businesses. By the virtue of hosting the Exposition, this injects millions of shillings into the MICE Economy in Kenya through various suppliers in Logistics, Accommodation, Booth Design and Supply, Branding, and Media. Hosting of similar events always have a ripple effect in the economy and to be specific the MICE value chain.
