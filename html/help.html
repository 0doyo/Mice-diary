How Can We Help You?

At MICE Diary, we are dedicated to providing the best support possible to help you navigate through any questions or concerns. Below are some of the ways you can reach out to us:

Email Support

Contact us at support@micediary.or.ke for any inquiries or support requests.

Live Chat

Our live chat support is available Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM. Click the chat icon on the bottom right of the page.

Phone Support

Call us at +254 123 456 789 for immediate support. Our lines are open Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 5 PM.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I register for an event on MICE Diary?

You can register for events by visiting our events page and selecting the event you wish to attend. Follow the registration instructions provided on the page.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, and follow the steps to reset your password.

How can I become a partner or sponsor?

For partnership and sponsorship opportunities, please email us at partnerships@micediary.or.ke.
